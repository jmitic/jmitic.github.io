Olympic GamesTokyo 2020

Swimming

  • The swimming competitions at the 2020 Summer Olympic Games in Tokyo were supposed to takeplace from 25 July to 6 August 2020.
    • Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the games have been postponed from 23 July to 8 August 2021.

Tokyo Aquatics Centre

Three Olympic records, and two World Records were broken. Events are:

  • Women 200m breastroke
  • Men 100m butterfly
  • Women 100m backstroke
  • Men 200m breastroke
  • Women 100m freestyle
United States finished with overall first place.
  • Most Valuable Swimmer.
    • Caleb Dressel
    • Katie Ledecky

The Olympics ended with the Americans in the usual position, on top of the Olympic swimming medal table.The Americans ended up with 30 total medals, a bit less than 33 from the 2016 Rio Games and 31 from the 2012 Olympics.Caeleb Dressel, of course, won five gold medals (three individual and two relay) for the USA.
