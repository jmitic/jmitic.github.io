Swimming

The swimming competitions at the 2020 Summer Olympic Games in Tokyo were supposed to take place from 25 July to 6 August 2020. However,due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the games have been postponed from 23 July to 8 August 2021. Their official name remained 2020 Summer Olympics.

Tokyo Aquatics Centre

The Olympics ended with the Americans in the usual position, on top of the Olympic swimming medal table.The Americans ended up with 30 total medals, a bit less than 33 from the 2016 Rio Games and 31 from the 2012 Olympics.Caeleb Dressel, of course, won five gold medals (three individual and two relay) for the USA.
