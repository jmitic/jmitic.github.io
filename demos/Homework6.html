More about Girls Who Code

Welcome to Girls Who Code! We are so excited that you're here! Now that you've joined us,— it means you're a part of a sisterhood of 300,000+ other awesome members who are using computer science to shape their world. Together, we're working to ensure that women have a greater role in building the technology of the future by creating inclusive spaces to learn Computer Science! We can't wait to introduce you to all of the interesting folks who are a part of this international movement

What makes Girls Who Code special is that we're not just here to teach you how to code. We want to help you become the most awesome, inventive, creative, curious, passionate, playful, resourceful, fun, and friendly computer scientists possible!

To do that, you can't just practice coding skills. If you want to be the best computer scientist possible, you will also have to grow some important strengths to help you and your projects succeed.

Coordinator for Girls Who Code at Converse University

My name is Jovana Mitic, and I am a junior majoring in Mathematics and minoring in Computer Science and Data Science at Converse University. I chose Girls Who Code because I wanted to get young girls into coding, which was something I was not introduced while growing up. I also wante to be part of the community of strong, intelligent, and supporting women who are making a difference and closing a gender gap.
